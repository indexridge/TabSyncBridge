Why bypass the cloud?

  • Total Privacy Your tab URLs stay in your local network. Zero external tracking or indexing.
  • Cross-Ecosystem Breaks the wall between Google Chrome on your desktop and Safari on your iPhone.
  • Instant Transfer WebSockets update tabs instantly when on the same network.
  • No Sign-ins No need to log into the same Google or Apple ID on both devices.

How it works

Install the iOS app and the free Chromium extension. Run the native bridge on your desktop, and they automatically discover each other via Bonjour/mDNS. Tabs sync seamlessly across devices without an internet connection.
