TabSyncBridge Privacy Choices

Effective date: May 5, 2026.

TabSyncBridge is designed so the developer does not collect tab titles, tab URLs, pairing state, linked device identifiers, or analytics from the app.

Clear Local Tab and Pairing Data

Use the remove linked profile controls in TabSyncBridge to remove old linked devices and remote tab state. You can also delete the app to remove app-local data stored by iOS.

Disable Local Network Access

On iPhone or iPad, open Settings, find TabSyncBridge, and turn off Local Network access. Sync cannot work while Local Network access is disabled.

Disable the Safari Extension

Open iOS Settings, go to Safari, then Extensions, and disable TabSyncBridge if you no longer want Safari tab sync.

Manage Subscription

Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and restore purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Manage subscriptions in your Apple Account subscription settings.

Support

For privacy questions, contact: TabSyncBridge@outlook.com.
