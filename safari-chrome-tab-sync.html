TabSyncBridge

One local view for Safari and Chromium tabs.

TabSyncBridge is for people who move between iPhone Safari and desktop Chromium-family browsers and want readable tab titles without using a cloud tab-sync account.

View on the App Store Chrome Web Store companion

Cross-browser pieces

  • iPhone and Safari The iOS app and Safari extension support Safari-side workflows and the shared tab list.
  • Chromium-family browsers The Chromium extension and desktop bridge support Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi, Chromium, and compatible browsers.
  • Readable tab titles Tab titles help you recognise pages quickly when switching devices.
  • Local control Pairing and sync are designed for devices you control on the same private local network.

Why this exists

Safari on iPhone and desktop Chromium-family browsers do not normally share a simple local tab-control surface. TabSyncBridge gives your own linked devices a focused private workflow for viewing, reconnecting, cleaning up old profiles, and closing selected remote tabs.

TabSyncBridge is £2.99 per month after a 7-day free trial through Apple StoreKit.
