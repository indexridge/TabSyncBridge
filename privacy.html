TabSyncBridge Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026.

TabSyncBridge is a local-network tab synchronization app. It helps you view and close browser tabs across your own linked devices.

App Store Privacy Label

For App Store privacy label purposes, TabSyncBridge does not collect data from this app. TabSyncBridge processes tab information locally and exchanges it only between your own linked devices on your private local network. The developer does not receive, store, or access this data through a developer server, analytics SDK, advertising SDK, tracking SDK, or third-party relay service.

Data TabSyncBridge Handles

TabSyncBridge may store or process browser tab titles, browser tab URLs, local device and browser profile identifiers, local pairing state, and subscription entitlement state provided by Apple StoreKit.

TabSyncBridge does not send tab titles, tab URLs, pairing secrets, or local device/profile identifiers to the developer.

How Data Is Used

This data is used to show your own tabs, connect your own devices, remove old linked profiles, preserve last known tab state, reconnect when network access is available, and route close-tab actions to the correct device.

Local-Only Sync

TabSyncBridge is designed for local-network sync. It does not require a cloud account for the core sync loop, and it does not use third-party relay servers for tab synchronization.

Chrome Web Store Limited Use

For the Chromium extension, use and transfer of information received from browser APIs is limited to providing TabSyncBridge's local-network tab sync and selected remote close features. TabSyncBridge does not use this information for advertising, profiling, credit-worthiness, or unrelated purposes, and does not sell it.

Analytics and Tracking

TabSyncBridge does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking SDKs.

TabSyncBridge does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Subscriptions

Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and restore purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store.
