TabSyncBridge

Private local tab sync for your own devices.

TabSyncBridge keeps browser tab visibility local: your iPhone, Safari, Chromium-family browsers, and linked desktop setup communicate across your own private local network.

View on the App Store Chrome Web Store companion

Built around local control

  • No cloud account for sync The core sync loop is designed for devices you control on the same private network.
  • Readable tab titles See tab titles from linked browsers instead of working from raw links alone.
  • Targeted close requests Close actions are routed to the browser profile that owns the selected tab.
  • Old profile cleanup Remove linked profiles that are no longer part of your setup.

What you need

Use the iPhone app with the Safari extension for Safari workflows. Use the Chromium extension and desktop bridge when linking Chrome, Edge, Brave, Opera, Vivaldi, or another compatible Chromium-family browser.

TabSyncBridge is £2.99 per month after a 7-day free trial. Purchases, renewals, cancellations, refunds, and restore purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store.
